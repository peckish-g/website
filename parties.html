How does Peckish create the ultimate dinner party?

Food for thought.

Whatever the occasion, Peckish looks to elevate and help transform the dinner party experience whilst still celebrating the simple joy of people sitting around a table enjoying good food and good company.

The quality of the food is just one element.  

Who - a simple and obvious one but a necessary starting point  .

Why - is it a special occasion, birthday, anniversary, old friends, work colleagues?  

Time of year - no one wants gazpacho when it’s -5 outside. The seasonality of produce and availability of flowers are also important considerations.

Table setting - depending on the above and budget, the theme will be reflected in the place cards, menus, flowers, lighting and so forth. 

Personal touches.

Genevieve's watercolour designs are available to buy and she also takes individual commissions for personalised pieces that incorporate calligraphy. Please do get in touch with any personalisation you might be interested in, whether it be for a dinner party, wedding or anything else you can think of!
